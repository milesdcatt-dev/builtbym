FAQ

Common questions.

Still on the fence? These are the ones we get on almost every call.

Are you actually 19?
Yes. Miles, Mason, and Max are all 19. We're putting ourselves through college by running real businesses. We mention it because it's the source of our pricing edge — no office, no payroll, no middle management.
What if I don't like the preview?
You haven't paid anything yet, so you walk away. The preview is free. If you like it, we keep building. If not, we shake hands and move on. No follow-ups, no pressure.
Do I own the website?
Yes. The domain is registered in your name, the site is yours. If you ever want to leave, we hand over the files and the domain transfers. We're confident enough in the service to not lock you in.
What if I already have a website?
We'll take a look and tell you honestly. Sometimes a refresh is the right move, sometimes a full rebuild. If your current site is already doing the job, we'll say so — we'd rather pass than sell you something you don't need.
How does the monthly fee work?
Flat rate, billed monthly. Covers hosting, SSL, domain renewals, backups, uptime monitoring, ongoing support, and one round of content updates per month (change a phone number, swap a photo, add a service — whatever). Cancel anytime.
How are you cheaper than agencies?
No office, no salespeople, no project managers, no junior devs we mark up. Three founders doing every job. Plus we built our own lead-gen tool, which means we don't spend on cold outreach. That math gets passed to you.
